The information you are about to receive is not for the weak of heart or those who care not about colonic health. I offer this information simply because I was amazed myself. It may or may not be healthy, you be the judge.

Yesterday, upon waking I ate a measured cup of Kashi GoLean (10g) and a banana (3g). I went to class, on the way to work I had an apple (3g). At lunchtime I had a turkey and cheese on wheat sandwich (4g). Pretzels (2g) a yogurt with a combination of rasberries and blueberries (3g). An orange (3g) as a midafternoon snack.

To this point we are at a very healthy 28 grams of fiber. But we haven't covered dinner yet.

I had a salad with two tomatoes (2g), two stalks of celery (2g) and red pepper (2g). There was more in the salad but that was all that was there with significant fiber content.

None of this is quite rediculous though. 34 grams of fiber in a day is alot but healthy. The kicker is the main course though. We made fajitas. I had two. Nothing in them of fibrous consequence right? Right. But the whole wheat tortillas we made the fajitas with weighed in at a whopping 21grams of fiber a piece. And I had two.

That's 76 grams of fiber for the day. Needless to say, everything is moving along smoothly today.
