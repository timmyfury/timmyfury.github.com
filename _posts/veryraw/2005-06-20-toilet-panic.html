I don't know if this happens to anyone else. It's been an issue for me all my life and I apologize for it ahead of time because this is perhaps too much information.

Sometimes I'll go into the bathroom and I'll lift the seat. As I am sitting down my leg will touch the cold edge of the porcelin bowl. I've lifted both the cover and the seat. This really shouldn't be an enormous issue but there's something way back in the back of my brain that totally freaks and my whole body spasms and I arch my back and stiffen up and stand at attention. My body involuntarily does anything it can to avoid putting my ass on that bowl rim.

It was never really a problem when I was younger because I was young and limber. Things are different now though. I'm old and large, stiff and out of shape. I hurt myself everytime.
