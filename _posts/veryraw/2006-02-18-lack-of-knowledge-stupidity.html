Although I hate being called a computer guy, and the assumption that I know about every aspect of computing, I frequently get questions about things that are not in my expertise. It's mostly because I'm approachable and for the most part I have the ability to answer a question about computers or technology without making people feel like idiots. What drives me crazy though is when someone comes to me and asks me a question, but prefaces the question with, "I know I'm totally stupid for asking this question but..."

This is easily one of the most annoying things in the world. I would like people to realize that my specialty, the thing I spend nearly every waking moment thinking about and one of my favorite things in the world is technology. The reason I know how to do all the things they want to know how to do is because technology is not only my job but also my hobby. I do not know how to teach English to people who can not speak it. I cannot diagnose sickness and I cannot put an IV in someones viens. I do not know how to paint or play the piano. I know technology.

I would like people to realize that a lack of knowledge about technology does not equal stupidity.

I feel that the thought is based in the old IT stereotype. When the helpdesk first became prominate, they were wizards and gods. They could make computers do things that others couldn't do. They took a long time and they were cocky and they made people feel like shit. This is not the way it should be and for the most part now this is not the way it is. Helpdesk now is as much help as it is comforting (at least it should be).

I think that IT people in general, whether you be a developer or a networking guy or tech support, should take extra care to make people feel as though its not their job to understand every intricacy of the technical world. I think that the technologically inclined of the world should adopt the attitude of care and comfort and help the other people of the world to realize that just because they don't know what they are doing does not mean they are stupid.
