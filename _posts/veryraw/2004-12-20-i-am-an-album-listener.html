Long ago when I first started amassing my ever growing music collection (mp3 collection which is nearing 9,000 songs). I was a song listener. I listened to individual songs with little rhyme or reason. In those days my musical taste was, Shall I say, immature and had not reached the level which it is now. I have become quite a music snob. Thanks alot Mark.

I digress

In those early days of naivety I had little thought of organization and little conscienseness of what I was listening to. With 300 songs to browse, there was little need. As my collection has grown though I have lost track of individual songs and they all become one big mass of data. The individual song has lost its importance.

Albums have become my way of distincting good from bad. I search for good albums now. Any retard with half a brain and three fingers can put out a good song. It takes a real artist to compose a 12 song masterpiece. A list of songs which speaks to me and tells me a story. A piece of works which holds my ever waning attention span. To move me. To make me feel something, good or bad.

I offer you this list of albums which I consider masterpieces. Feel free to offer yours as well. If you are interested in hearing any of these, let me know and maybe you can download them borrow the CD from me.

  • Underworld - Beaucoup Fish
  • Lovage - Music to Make Love to Your Old Lady By
  • Ultraviolence - Life of Destructor
  • The Mars Volta - De-Loused in the Comatorium
  • Kid Loco - A Grand Love Story
  • Shirley Bassey - The Remix Album... Diamonds Are Forever
  • Venetian Snares - Finding Candace
  • Coldplay - Parachutes
  • Radiohead - Kid A
  • Tom Waits - Anything by Tom Waits, I mean come on, he's Tom Waits
