Tomorrow I will be going to An Event Apart, a one day conference about Web Standards. Two great names in standards will be there. Eric Meyer and Jeffrey Zeldman are standards celebrities that I have been reading since I got into standards almost three years ago. I am probably more excited for this event than I should be (I already have my clothes layed out), but this is not only my first web conference, but its at my favorite place on earth, The Franklin Institute, to boot.

I will be blogging from the event elsewhere. I'm joining Jeff and James and a few others on the Philadelphia Standards Organization blog alongside the fellows of Pixelworthy in documenting the events and the learning and the fun. If you are in to this sort of thing or just love my awesome writing, stop over and visit the blog to see what's going on.
