Just got the word that my car is ready for me to pick up. That's great. Had it inspected today and had to replace the entire rear brake setup. $600 inspection. Plus the $250 for the new tires and alignment this weekend. Could end up being $200 more if I can't get the two tickets I got for expired inspection thrown out. It seems like every year I get nailed on my inspection. Last year, right after buying the car I threw down nearly $1200 for inspection. The year before it was $1400 for the big blue ford I was driving then.

Something tells me that my affinity for the $400 car is going to grow old someday. At which point I will need to either buy a new car (a whole new can of misery for sure) or move into the city and do away with my car altogether. Who knows. For now I will just deal. All in all the car has cost me only $2500 total. Not too bad.
