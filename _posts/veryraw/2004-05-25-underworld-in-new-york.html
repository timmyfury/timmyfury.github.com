I have pretty much listened to nothing but Underworld for about three weeks. I really don't think I will ever get bored of them. For those of you who have never heard of them, they are close to my favorite band. They make the most amazing, emotion inspiring music you have ever heard. (Just short of the Moulin rouge soundtrack, eh Mark?) Don, Jenny, Trish and I went to see them in New York City almost two years ago and it was the most amazing show. It was even better than U2's Popmart tour. The fucking show just built and built for nearly two hours until they played Born Slippy and the whoop drops and its all dent,dent,dent,dent... dent,dent,dent,dent... boh,boh... Gives me chills just thinking about it. I danced my arse off that night and still get mocked occasionally for looking like a doof when I dance. Here was the playlist for the night.

  1. Skyscraper
  2. Cowgirl / Rez
  3. Two Months Off
  4. Dinosaur Adventure 3d
  5. Twist
  6. Dirty Epic
  7. Mo Move
  8. Dark Train
  9. Improvisation
  10. Jumbo
  11. Born Slippy NUXX
  12. Push Upstairs
  13. Pearl's Girl
  14. Moaner

If you have not had the pleasure of hearing Underworld perhaps you will instant message me (MyMouthIsAToilet or TimmyFury on AIM) and I will share with you.
