Using aerial photos, officials have found a 400 foot waterfall in California. I should say they didn't just find it. They had apparently found it then lost it, then found it again. As I read the article on MSNBC I thought to myself, How the hell do you lose a 400 foot waterfall. Then, on further inspection of this article from National Geographic, I realized how.

The waterfall is called Whiskeytown Falls, located in Whiskeytown National Recreation Area. Hmmm... I wonder.
