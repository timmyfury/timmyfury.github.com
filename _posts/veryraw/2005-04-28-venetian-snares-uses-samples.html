I listened to the new Venetian Snares album, Winnepeg is a frozen shithole breifly in Mark's car today. Really good. I haven't had a chance to "buy it" myself but after the amazing breaks and hard bass of the new album, I had a hankerin' for some sweet sweet Venetian Snares.

There is one particular song that I have always thought was really good in addition to making me laugh my ass off. The samples in this song are amazing. I don't even know where someone would go about getting samples like this. I have discussed this song with Mark before and I don't know if we ever came to a consensus or not. The song is in my computer as "Venetian Snares - Doormouse Megamix." This is where the point of contention was I think. If anyone else knows anything about this, let me know.
