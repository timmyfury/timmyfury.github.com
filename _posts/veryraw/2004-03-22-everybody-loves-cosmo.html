So I was walking around my house tonight making suction cup noises with my mouth as I walked. I thought it was really funny. I mean imagine if I had suction cups on my feet. It would obviously rule. You could totally walk up walls and pick things up without bending over. I mean some people can already do this but I mean you could do it without being called awful names like "Crazy Long toe McGee" or "Finger Foot".

Then this made me think back to the good old days. Back when my house was rockin' an IBM PS/2 486. Man did we rule. We bought this shareware game, called "Cosmo's Cosmic Adventures." Circa March 1992. I loved that game. In fact I loved it so much that I still have it. It originally came on one 3.5" floppy and took like 3 minutes to load. That was on windows3.1. Now I have moved up in the world and I have me this fancy pentium2.3ghz with close to a gig of RAM driving around with a full load of software on Windows XP Pro. Only took me three days to install on this machine and close to 25 minutes to get the damn thing running but man is this sweet.

Look at this guy. It would rule. You could drink as much as you want and never drop your glass. You could climb buildings and shit. You would rule the window washing business. I do suppose there would be downfalls though. There's no way in hell you could manage driving without some sort of special apparatus. Changing your spark plugs may be difficult. Certainly you would have extreme trouble with your girlfriend's extra-curriculars. Although I suppose it would make a serious penis enlargement system. It could also make a great portable cereal bowl. But not at the same time I suppose.

To sum up what I am trying to say, giant suction cups have benefits and fallbacks just like hands do.
