I have noticed every single time I drive past that there is this giant chainsaw on the construction site. So yeah, you're like, "A giant chainsaw, whatever." But I'm definately like, "Look at the damn pictures." That's a god damn giant chainsaw. This thing mystifies me. It looks freakin' awesome. It's apparently made by Case, I believe its actually a trencher. But it certainly looks like a giant chainsaw to me. I mean come on. Look at the picture of Mark right next to it. He's near six foot and he looks like a midget. The blade on this thing is like thirty feet long and five foot high. I will now offer you a few simple scenerios for reasons that you would need to own on of these enormous chainsaws...
  • "Honey, that tree in the backyard really sizzles my nipples. Do you think that we could remove it this weekend?"
  • "I'm installing these beautiful new French doors in my house but this dang sawz-it-all just won't make it through the walls."
  • "My wife is pregnant, traffic is terrible and my monster truck is broken down. Can I borrow your car?"
  • "I'll show those bloody neighbors for playing there music loudly"
  • "I'm performing a nation wide survey. Do you have five minutes to answer a few short questions?"
  • "Hey John, my carving knife seems to have gone missing. Could you please offer me some sort of turkey carving implement?"
  • "Oh great and wise moat digger. The Visigoths are advancing on the western stretches of our vast kingdom. His royal highness has requested you by name. He desires a moat around the shire. Can you do it?"
You see? The giant chainsaw has tons of useful household applications.
