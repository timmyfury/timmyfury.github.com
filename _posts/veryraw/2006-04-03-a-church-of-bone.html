Every now and then I catch something on flickr that makes me stop and say wow. Thecnote has posted a set of images from the Sedlec Ossuary in the Czech Republic. As the surrounding cemetary became more popular, the land became full and bodies were exhumed and the bones stacked in this ossuary. My favorites? The bone chandelier and the bone garland.
