Trish and I arrived from the gym tonight and someone was parked in Burn's spot. As I walked past his car I noticed that there was someone in the car and they were surfing myspace on a laptop. I'm walking to the door and realize, that guy is skimming off my wireless connection. Looks like we got ourselves a war driver. So we go inside and unplug the modem and sure enough when he was finished with the page he was reading, he clicked on another link and realized that the large man staring at him from the doors in front of him had disconnected him.

With him gone I logged into the wireless router we use to connect Trish to the network in our apartment. I locked that shit down tight. Limit by MAC address, limit number of IP's, encrypt the shit out of it, password the wireless, password the network, rename the network, you name it, if it was an option, I did it. We were lucky though. This guy was really only out for a quick myspace fix. Luckily he wasn't out for a quick, charge the shit out of my credit card fix.

Moral of the story, lock your fucking network down.
