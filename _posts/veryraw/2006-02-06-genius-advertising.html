The insinuation in this advertisement that the H3 is the offspring of a monster and a robot makes me want one. But the marketing campaign as a whole is really well done. Jennifer the monster (beware sound) has a website. On the website you can download her music from her DJ sessions. I love the mid 90's styling of the site. The fake animated gifs and the awesome repeating background. Robot has a website too. ROBOTastic Enterprises, Inc. is equally well designed and hilarious. All around a good advertising campaign. If their objective was to reach computer nerds who enjoy bad internet humor, mission accomplished.

In other news, check out the google videos page of superbowl commercials. Here are some of my favorites.

  • Michelob Amber - Touch Football - Reminds me of the Terry Tate, the office linebacker commercials from a few years ago.
  • Bud Light - Hidden Bud Light - You can never go wrong with office violence.
  • Dove - Campaign for Real Beauty - Okay call me softee but I thought this commercial was really touching. What an incredibly noble effort by Dove. Maybe we will have less self-conscience freaks running around. (senstitive, not too sensitive)
  • Vault - Scarecrow laser shooting robots? Need I say more?
  • Burger King - Whopperettes - Girls dressed as food, piling on top of each other to create a delicious sandwich. Plus the king. Little bit disturbing. Lotta bit fun.
