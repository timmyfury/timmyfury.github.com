When you tie a $60 tie, you get a nice meaty knot. At the end of the day you still have that meaty knot. The tie looks exactly the same at the end of the day. So don't spare expense on your ties. You can get cheap shoes, pants and shirts if your budget doesn't warrant expensive clothes. You will still look good. People don't notice these things. People always notice your tie. If you cheap out on your ties, everyone will know that you cheaped out on your ties.
