I stopped at the bank yesterday to deposit some loot and saw that the teller had some Eisenhower dollars behind the counter.

Tim: May I buy some of those Eisenhower dollars?
Teller: Sure how many do you want?
Tim: How many your got? (I question as if I'm some high roller by insinuating that I'd buy all that they have there.)
Teller: 225. (she states smuggly)
Tim: (defeated) I'll take five.

I don't collect these things. I have several in a collection of many other coins, US and international, but I didn't buy these to add to the collection. I got them so I could use them.

My father often asks for Eisenhower dollars, Sacagawea Golden Dollars, Susan B. Anthony dollars, JFK fifty cents pieces and two dollar bills when he's at the bank for the same reason. It's fun to use them if for no other reason than to see people's reactions.
