To follow up that entry which was horribly boring, I would like to offer a peace offering.

If you could have any super power, what would it be? Here are mine.

  • Background music and sound effects - No matter what I was doing everyone would hear music that matched the mood and sound effects that made everything I did more emphatic. No one would be able to react to them (ie dancing or looking around trying to figure out where it was coming from) it would just be there. Only for me.
  • Spit bees - I would open my mouth and bees would fly out with the pressure of a fire hose. I would be immune but everyone would be subjected to the horror of my mouth bees.

What are yours?
