Tomorrow I will be shaving off my beard. I have been thinking about doing it for a while now. What's held me up though is that I didn't know what to do with all the hair I've been saving on my face since August. I was planning on going sideburnless. I haven't been without sideburns since 1999. A long time. It sort of troubled me. No sideburns? Just sick. I've decided to keep them though. Tomorrow when I shave my beard I will retain a bit of said beard in a mean pair of sideburns. I've also decided to do something I have been thinking about doing for a while. I'm going to document the changes in my facial hair over the course of the summer. There will no doubt be plenty of terrible shapes in them and I think that you should all get to enjoy them as much as I will.
