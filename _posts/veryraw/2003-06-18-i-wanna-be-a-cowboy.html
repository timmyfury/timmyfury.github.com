What else hasn't changed is my bizarre and somewhat crude drawing style. I have more of my first grade drawings and you will see in a few days that my sense of humor then was as outlandish as it is now. Anyway. Mark says that I am a prophet. He thinks the ghost behind me is a glum sars cloud. It would be glum because of how inaffective it is as a killer. So the prophecy would be 'when I start dressing like a cowboy, an insignificant disease will sweep the land and kill very few.' Being that I wear a big texas belt buckle, own several cowboy hats and ride a horse to work everyday. We are doing probabilities in class. We were studying dice and the probabilities of them and Mr. Maruca says, "...you can't roll higher then a 6, we aren't playing dungeons and dragons." I could almost hear the record scratch.
