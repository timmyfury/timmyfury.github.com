I've grown quite fond of biking recently. I have gone on several good treks which some may have seen in the side list of links. As my interest in biking has grown I have also been reading Tour de France news. Interesting stuff. I also heard a story about how Lance Armstrong's oversized heart and excessively efficient muscles allow him to trudge up the Alps during the tour. Now I can't stop thinking about Lance. I read as much as can about him. I look at pictures of him. It's creepy. I have even caught myself dropping Lance trivia into conversations and going as far as saying "Lance would never do that," as if I knew him.

Anyway, for those of you who are not eerily obsessed with Lance but would like to see some really bizarre happenings at the tour, check out the first half of this photoset.
