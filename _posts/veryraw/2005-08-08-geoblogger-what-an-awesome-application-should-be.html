Geoblogger is a site that displays your flickr images on a map. It knows where to display your images by the longitude and latitude tags that you add to your images. If you want to know more about the specifics, try the geobloggers website. Dan says it better that I.

So I've been doing this for a little while now, but a new interface for geobloggers was realeased recently, and its really quite nice. I'm totally loving the way it operates. Even better is the fellow that mantains the site. Not only did Dan take the time to answer my messages to him about the site but he went out of his way to show me how to craft the URL's to show my images only and control the way it appears to you. Dan went way out of his way for someone who is maintaining this site as a hobby and for free. An upstanding chap for sure.

Try checking out my map of images that I have geotagged.
