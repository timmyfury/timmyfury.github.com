I was brushing my teeth the other day. I had noticed that the bristles were a bit worn and it should probably be replaced, but I waited. I waited too long. In the middle of brushing my teeth, every bristle in the brush fell out of the tooth brush in my mouth leaving behind a bare handle. It was bizarre, terrifying even. It's like driving down the road and the steering wheel falling off in your hands.

I looked in the linen closet and there was luckily an unused toothbrush there. It was "soft". The handle was flexible like a bendy straw. When I had finished brushing, my mouth felt no cleaner than before. It was weird. Don't even know why I bothered. I had to go out and buy another one of my trusty "medium" brushes. the Oral B Advantage. I have been using it for years. I have bought several different types of brushes as my secondary (for travel or campin) and nothing holds up quite like it.
