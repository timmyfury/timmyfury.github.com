Jarrod, Mark, Geoff, Michelle and I were rolling around yesterday in my parents van (the beast) because my driverside window is broken. Gets fixed tomorrow hopefully and all will be well. Thats not why I write though. We went to one shopping center and spent four hours there. How I don't know, but four hours none the less. Three stores. The third of which was the best. This new store is called fiVeBELoW. I swear to you that whent hey were building it Trish and I suspected that it could be a nice restaurant or something. Nope. Been wrong before, I'll be wrong again. It's like a dollar store but not. It's easily the greatest store ever. Tons of candy. Tons of cheap toys. A table top Pac-Man. Here's what they say...

Five Below is a new retail concept positioned to become the leading extreme-value retailer to the teen market and beyond. Five Below offers thousands of trend-right products for teens, as well as pre-teens and other value-minded consumers, all of which are priced from $1.00 to $5.00. source: About Five Below

The greatest thing that we got there was these foam darts.There's a thick rubber band through them. You put your finger through a loop at the end of one of the rubber bands and pull it back like a slingshot. They fly like mad. They were two for a dollarat fiVeBELoW. I bought four and it was the best two dollars I have ever spent. We used them for like 5 hours total yesterday. Get some of them. You won't regret it.
