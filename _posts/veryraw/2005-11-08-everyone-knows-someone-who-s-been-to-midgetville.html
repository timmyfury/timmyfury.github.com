5ives is one of my favorite sites. A few months ago He wrote a list called Five favorite rumors of my childhood. Here's the list...

  • Bubble Yum is made from spiders’ eggs
  • Mikey died from mixing Pop-Rocks and Coke
  • That one really weird teacher is “a gay”
  • People get beheaded on Space Mountain, literally, every day
  • One time, Rod Stewart drank a gallon of semen and totally had to have his stomach pumped

I loved this cause I remember I had a few of these. This guy must be a bit older than me cause for me it was Lil' Kim not Rod Stewart. I knew a girl in elementary school who wouldn't eat butterfingers because she thought they were made with crushed glass. Someone else I know told me that she thought when you whistle, the angels cry. I also had a friend, Bobby used to just make things up. Anything he wanted. We were building a fort once behind this guys house and there was a stack of fences in his back yard. Bobby convinced us to steal them because he knew that the guy who owned the house was deaf and blind from a fire in his house.

Anyway, Trish and I were talking to a friend of her's a few weeks ago and she brought up Midgetville. For those who don't know, Midgetville is a made up town. When I was little everyone knew someone who had been there but they had never been there themselves. Small houses. The midgets would always run out of their houses and throw rocks at your car. I thought it was in Springfield. Trish said near Taylor Hospital. Michelle said it was near the Granite Run Mall. The details were different for everyone.

Do you remember any irrational fears or bizarre misconceptions from your childhood? Where was midgetville for you? Do they throw rocks there?
