So I waited an hour and a half for the cable guy to come to my apartment. Didn't need to be here. The heavily accented gentleman who took my order on Sunday must have misunderstood what I wanted. They came in to put in digital cable. I didn't want it. I wanted standard. All I need is CNN, Discovery and the History Channel and I'm set. So I didn't need to be here when they came in. No biggy really. They go to the pole and set me up.

The point to my story here is that when the guys come in they are telling me I am getting digital cable, I tell them, "No I just need Discovery and the History Channel." The guys look at each other then look around the place. Dual screens. Metal playing. National geographic. Wired. Reading about Burning man. Two full bookshelves. SUSE linux book on the coffee table. I know exactly what they were thinking.

"A nerd lives here!"
