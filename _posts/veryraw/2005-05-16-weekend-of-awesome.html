I had an amazing weekend. The synopsis and a bit of detail in bulleted form with a multitude of accompanying links...

  • Back out of the city. A stop at Jarrod's house puts Trish and Michelle on the trampoline for about an hour.

A satisfying weekend to say the least. Feel free to talk amongst yourselves about how interesting and awesome my life is and I will moderate as I see fit.
