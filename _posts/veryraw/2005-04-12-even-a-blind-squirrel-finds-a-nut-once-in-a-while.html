In my attempts to pimp flickr to every person I know, I have been thinking alot about photography and pictures. One of the realizations I have come to is that I am not a photographer. I like to take pictures. I like to look at pictures. I even like to show my pictures to people. But I'm not a photographer. Although this is sort of a depressing realization for such a total wanna-be, its been sort of liberating. I shoot and shoot and shoot and no matter how crappy the world may think the images are, I share them anyway. You figure if I shoot a thousand pictures, eventually one of them has to catch someone's eye.

I may not be a photographer, but sometimes an image just needs a little help to become something really cool. Of course photoshop never hurt no one. so a little doctoring may be in order.

Below is one of those images. This is an the Antonov cargo plane that came into Philadelphia International while I was working for USAir. It was awesome. Freaking huge.

I removed some of the debris and sharpened the image a bit and I think it improved the image alot.
