As I noted a few weeks ago, I have been busy with my move. Trish and I have been in our new place for a week now and things are really getting together. I am finally hanging pictures and mirrors after some painting and organizing. We have filled the dumpster for our building twice now with things we are throwing away and the best part, I went out and bought a grill last night. Life is good.

The move has really initiated a life style change of sorts. I call this my quality of life move. Not only am I living with Trish now, which lets me see her more often, but it also cuts all driving times significantly. I have a 5 minute drive to work now. Instead of getting home around 6:30, I'm finishing dinner around 6:30. This obviously gives me a lot more time at night to do what I want at night. Furthermore, I am not making three trips a week to Trish's house a whopping 40 miles from my house.

The other difference is that with a refridgerator full of food and beer, I end up eating out less. Very healthy. I actually feel better physically. and I eat more regularly.

Then there's the coffee pot. My god. How nice is it to have a coffee pot in your place. Cutting my drive from an hour to five minutes in the morning has given me 55 minutes to sit in my apartment and enjoy a half of a pot of coffee before I go to work. Drinking coffee in your underwear is always better than drinking it in your car on the way to work.

I'll take some pictures of the place and post them so you can see what I'm living like.
