Yesterday I was walking to my class and I saw this girl standing in the middle of the courtyard holding a string that was attached to an enormous floating sausage. Seriously. It was huge. Like 3 feet around and 25 feet long. A serious kielbasa. Oh and it was floating.... Like in the air.
