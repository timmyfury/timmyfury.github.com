So I was listening to the radio the other day and the commentator said, "Something-something-something-burgled-something-something." Burgle being the act of a burglar. What the hell kind of word is that really. I mean do you really think you would be able to take a police officer seriously if you came home, saw a bunch of police action out front of your house and then the officer says, "Ma'am or Sir, your house has been burgled." Really now.

This leads me to a theory that I have. Perhaps if we renamed all the crimes in the world with silly names, they wouldn’t seem so terrible. We could call rape snorklebonking.
