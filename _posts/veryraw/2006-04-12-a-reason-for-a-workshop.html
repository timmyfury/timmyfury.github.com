A few weeks ago Mark and I were discussing owning a home reasons to purchase a home... individually... without the involvement of each other... in any way. I want to buy a house so I can set up a workshop. We really couldn't think of any practical reasons to have a workshop though since I don't do any carpentry or anything. Well I think I may have something.

I have always been fascinated by chain reaction displays. There is one at The Franklin Institute that I could watch for hours. Check this one out, Incredible Machines. Seems like a good hobby to take up. Combines physics, little bit of engineering, creativity, tinkering and gives you a reason to buy toys and build up a solid collection of junk.
