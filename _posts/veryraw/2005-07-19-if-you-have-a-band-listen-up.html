I found this band yesterday that I really like. Gay for Johnny Depp. They are pretty good, remind me of The Blood Brothers. So I do a search for them and I come up with there website. Its freaking horrible. A goddamned nightmare. Let me tell you whats wrong with the site...

  • The name of the band is not even on the site. I wasn't sure if it was even the bands website.
  • Frames. Bloody frames. Stop fucking using frames. You're not hiding anything from me, I will get what I want from your site if I want it. Frames will not stop me.
  • Flash music player. Whats the point. Offer me music to download. I don't want to listen to it on your rotten website.
  • Creative non-descript titles for your links. They aren't creative really, I don't know what they are. I don't know how to find the content I want.
  • The worst offense of all. How do I buy your fucking album. The sites I am refered to are un-navigable. I can't find your album anywhere. Why would I ever buy an album from a store I can't find anything in.

After all this I still want to buy the album but I don't know how. I tried amazon but got no where. Putting your music on amazon isn't selling out, its smart. Why would you make your music so difficult to find.

Just for prosperity sake I should mention a few other things that bother me in addition to those mentioned above about band websites...

  • Stop using Flash. I can't link to a part of your site if you are using flash. It makes it impossible to navigate and I don't bloody care how many things you can get to fly across my screen in like three seconds. Handsome Boy Modeling School is a classic example of the overuse of Flash. If you click that link there is a good chance your computer will come to a stand still and your browser will crash.
  • Don't put a link up to content then put an under construction page there. It's so annoying. I hate in the off occasion that I can actually find what I want on a band site the content isn't actually there. The Blood Brothers are not only a great example of this (try downloading a song or two), but they also use worthless un-navigable flash.
  • Offer me some sample music. I mean full songs. Snippets never get stuck in my head and make me hunt your album down.
  • Give me metadata. Before you upload those songs to your site, make sure the metadata is proper so I can find it again in my music player later. If I can't find it, I can't listen to it again and again, therefore it will never get stuck in my head and force me to buy your album. I downloaded two songs from the Yeah Yeah Yeahs website last night and they had no metadata. It took me like five minutes to find it again once I imported it into my itunes library.

This is certainly not a comprehensive list. This is merely a list of the things that irritate me most about band sites. When I go to a band site, I want to know history, lyrics, discography and I want to download media. Give me information on your site. Your music is your art, not your website.
