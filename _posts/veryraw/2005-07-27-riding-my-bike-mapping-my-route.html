As mentioned before in my entry about the amazing Lance Armstrong, I have been riding my bike. Since I moved to Media I have been getting out more to ride. Using this great Google maps application called GMaps Pedometer I can track my ride turn for turn and see how far I have traveled. I was just saving the links on del.icio.us but I decided last night to screenshot them and upload them to flickr where I can make notes and comments about the course I rode as well as dates and important areas to remember when mapping out future routes to take on my rides. Anyway for those who are interested you can take a look at my maps. They are in my Bike Routes set.
