On Sunday, Trish, Mark Geoff and I took a ride out to Birdsboro, PA for the Pagoda Motorcycle Club Junk Car Race to watch my old blue Crown Victoria get beat on. It was a really good time and we sure learned a thing or two about rural America. I thought the car would get destroyed but it didn't do bad at all. Ends up that Kim and Jason are going to use the car again next year. Awesome.

This is going to be a two part post. This one has all the digital images that Trish took while I shot with film. I have to wait for the film to be developed and then I have to scan them. Chances are you won't get those images for a while. These are Trish's touched up a bit in photoshop by me.

These images have all been moved into a flickr set.
