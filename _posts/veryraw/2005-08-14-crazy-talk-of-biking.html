There's been a bit of crazy talk on Brian's blog. And it was continued in the comments of my last entry. The idea is a cross country ride. A tour from ocean to ocean. John Dorsey has written a really extensive account of his first trip across and the adventure that the idea offers is nearly more than I can handle. It sounds exciting and fun.

There are obvious issues though. I am currently about 50 pounds outside of the weight I would need to be to make a ride like that. John says that the only thing that can prepare you for six weeks of riding 75 miles a day is six weeks of riding 75 miles a day, but I must say that being in relatively proper shape from the start will make it easier. I figure two years will be a proper amount of time to train for something like this. Weights and eating right and all.

I need to do some reasearch on bikes and get a new lighter bike. Mine currently weighs about 35 pounds. I should probably get a lighter one if I am going to strap 100 pounds of gear to it. I also need to get my endurance up and take some really long rides. I would like to make the ride to King of Prussia sometime in September. I would like to plan a few other long rides and by next June (or there abouts) I would like to make a really long, multiple day journey. New York City to Philadelphia and later Boston to Philadelphia or Washington.

There is also this City to Shore bike tour that I think I could probably participate in by the end of September if I worked hard through August and the beginning of September. Thinking about it but not sure.
