Mark calls it meatspace. I think its funny. That's where I've been, meatspace. My updates have not only been sporatic but a little non-sequitor over the last week or so. For that I apologize (I think there are readers out there), but thats the story of life. It's summer and it's beautiful.

Michael is on vacation renovating his new house. So I am holding down the fort on my own. Not that it takes much work to do. Furthermore, I am in process of working on a few projects which I am very excited about. More on those later though. I don't want to talk about them before I have made any significant progress.

So the moral of my story is that meatspace is calling me. I'm enjoying my bike, my friends, my woman and my work. I've got alot going on right now and you'll all have to be patient over the next few weeks until life settles down to a nice slow pace again.
